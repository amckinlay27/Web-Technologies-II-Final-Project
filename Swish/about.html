About Swish

Selling only high quality can openers that offer exquisite craftsmanship and ingenious innovative design. At SWISH, we test every product to ensure the product’s quality, user friendly, innovative, and safe, ensuring a memorable experience with your can opener. Much like your phone, a can opener is a tool, you wouldn’t want a faulty phone. Which is why we take such pride in our services, offering an extended year warranty on top of the manufactures, our way of saying thank you for letting our products into your home and welcoming you to the extended SWISH family.

Our Story

Our story is unlike most, dating back to the 1860’s. It was a few years after the first can opener was created in the United States. When word spread to the great white north, Alexander McKinlay could not help but take notice. A craftsman and mathematician whose passion for intelligent design would lead him to a career in can openers. His eye for intricate detail and their mechanics helped lead him to the most efficiently designed can openers. He would acquire the can openers early, running extensive test to prove their worth. Once proved he would walk around his home tome, selling door to door.

Alexander McKinlay’s eye for detail has carried over for over the past century. Testing the designs to their limitations, using advance technology to determine quality, and only selling the best to our customers. A lot has changed over the past century, but our guarantee remains intact, Quality, Dedication, Family. Values that we have carried with us for the past 100 years, values we will carry for the next 100.
